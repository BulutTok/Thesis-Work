Assistant: To successfully graduate from the Master of Science program at the University of North Carolina Wilmington, there are several additional graduation requirements you should be aware of beyond completing the required credits:

Additional Graduation Requirements

1. Apply to Graduate:
You must apply to graduate via SeaNet by the specified deadline. This application is essential for your graduation to be processed.

2. Enrollment in Semester of Graduation:
Ensure you are enrolled in courses during the semester you intend to graduate. This maintains your active student status during the graduation process.

3. Research Project or Thesis Requirements:
If completing a research project:
Successfully defend your research project.
Complete all aspects of your final project.
If completing a thesis, fulfill these requirements:
Pass a comprehensive oral exam.
Defend your thesis.
Submit a draft thesis electronically to the Graduate School by the deadline.
Submit necessary forms such as Format Approval Sheet and Signed Thesis Title Page with your final thesis.
Submit an Electronic Thesis Form along with your final thesis submission.

Summary

Make sure you are enrolled during your intended graduation semester, apply for graduation on SeaNet before the deadline, and complete any remaining project or thesis requirements including defenses and submissions. Regular consultation with your academic advisor is recommended to ensure all requirements are met and nothing is overlooked.

For more detailed instructions or further assistance, visit [Graduate School Forms](https://uncw.edu/myuncw/academics/graduate-school/forms) page or contact the Registrar's Office directly.
