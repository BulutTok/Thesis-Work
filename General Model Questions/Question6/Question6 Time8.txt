Assistant: Graduation Requirements

To ensure you are fully prepared to graduate, there are several additional requirements beyond completing the required credits. Here's a structured breakdown:

Academic and Administrative Requirements
Apply to Graduate: You must apply to graduate in SeaNet by the specified deadline.
Enrollment Requirement: You must be enrolled in the semester you plan to graduate.

Research or Thesis Requirements (if applicable)
Research Project:
Defense of Project: Successfully defend your research project.
Final Project Completion: Complete all aspects of your final project.
Thesis (if applicable):
Comprehensive Oral Exam: Pass a comprehensive written exam.
Thesis Defense: Successfully defend your thesis.
Draft Thesis Submission: Submit a draft thesis electronically to the Graduate School by the deadline.
Format Approval Sheet: Submit a Format Approval Sheet with your draft thesis.
Final Thesis Submission: Submit a final thesis electronically by the deadline.
Signed Thesis Title Page: Provide a signed thesis title page with your final submission.
ETD Submission Form: Submit an Electronic Thesis Form with your final thesis.

Summary

Ensure that you meet all credit requirements and maintain enrollment during your graduation semester. Additionally, complete any necessary applications through SeaNet and fulfill all research or thesis obligations if they pertain to your program. Make sure you check specific deadlines for submissions and applications as part of these processes. If further clarification is needed, contacting the Registrar's Office or consulting with your academic advisor is recommended.
