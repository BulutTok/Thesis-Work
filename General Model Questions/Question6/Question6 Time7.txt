Assistant: Graduation Requirements for Master of Science in Computer Science & Information Systems

To ensure you are prepared to graduate, beyond completing the required credits, you need to fulfill several additional graduation requirements:

1. Apply to Graduate:
You must apply to graduate through SeaNet by the specified deadline. This is an essential step in notifying the university of your intent to complete your degree.

2. Enrollment Requirement:
You must be enrolled in the semester you intend to graduate. This ensures you are active within the university system and can officially complete your degree requirements.

3. Research Project or Thesis Requirements:
Defense of Project: Successfully defend your research project.
Final Project Completion: Complete and submit your final project.
If applicable, follow these thesis-specific steps:
Comprehensive Oral Exam: Pass a comprehensive written exam.
Thesis Defense Requirement: Successfully defend your thesis.
Draft Thesis Submission: Submit a draft thesis electronically by the deadline.
Format Approval Sheet: Submit this with your draft thesis.
Final Thesis Submission: Submit a final version of your thesis electronically by the deadline.
Signed Thesis Title Page: Include this with your final thesis submission.
ETD Submission Form: Complete and submit this form along with your final thesis.

Summary
Ensure all coursework and credit requirements (36 credits) are completed.
Apply for graduation on SeaNet before deadlines.
Be enrolled during your graduation semester.
Complete all research or thesis-related submissions and defenses as applicable.

These steps will help ensure that you meet all necessary criteria for successful completion of your Master's program at UNCW. For any specific questions about forms or detailed processes, refer to [Graduate School Forms](https://uncw.edu/myuncw/academics/graduate-school/forms).
