Assistant: Graduation Requirements for Master of Science at UNCW

To successfully graduate with a Master of Science degree, you need to be aware of and complete the following additional requirements beyond completing your required credits:

1. Apply to Graduate:
You must apply to graduate via SeaNet by the designated deadline. Ensure you are familiar with the specific deadlines for application as they can vary.

2. Enrollment in Semester of Graduation:
You must be enrolled in courses during the semester you intend to graduate. This is a requirement even if you've completed all coursework previously.

3. Research Project or Thesis Requirements:
Defense of Project: Successfully defend your research project.
Final Project Completion: Complete and submit the final project.
For those pursuing a thesis:
Comprehensive Oral Exam: Pass this exam as part of your thesis requirements.
Thesis Defense: Successfully defend your thesis before a committee.
Draft Thesis Submission: Submit an electronic draft thesis to the Graduate School by their deadline.
Format Approval Sheet: Submit this along with your draft thesis.
Final Thesis Submission: Submit the final version electronically by the deadline.
Signed Thesis Title Page: Include this with your final submission.
ETD Submission Form: Submit an Electronic Thesis Form along with your final thesis.

4. Additional Requirements:
Ensure that all necessary forms and submissions are completed through the [Graduate School Forms](https://uncw.edu/myuncw/academics/graduate-school/forms) page.

Conclusion
It is crucial to meet these administrative requirements alongside completing your academic credits to ensure a smooth path to graduation. Be sure to keep track of all deadlines and required documents, and consult with your academic advisor if you have any questions or need further clarification on any step in this process.
