Assistant: Strategies to Improve Your GPA

1. Course Selection
Focus on Core Courses: Aim for strong grades in remaining core courses like CSC 544, MIS 555, and MIS 565.
Balance Workload: Choose a mix of challenging and manageable courses.

2. Academic Support
Tutoring Services: Use university tutoring services for challenging subjects.
Study Groups: Join study groups for collaborative learning.

3. Time Management
Prioritize Tasks: Use planners to manage deadlines effectively.
Set Realistic Goals: Break assignments into smaller tasks with achievable goals.

4. Engage with Professors
Office Hours: Attend office hours regularly for help.
Feedback on Assignments: Seek feedback to understand areas of improvement.

5. Extra Resources
Online Courses & Workshops: Enroll in additional courses or workshops for extra practice.
Library Resources: Utilize library materials for further reading and research.

6. Health & Well-being
Stress Management: Practice stress-relief techniques like meditation or exercise.
Adequate Rest & Nutrition: Ensure sufficient rest and a balanced diet.

Academic Plan
Complete remaining core courses: CSC 544, MIS 555, MIS 565.
Engage in research or thesis work by enrolling in CSC/MIS 594 or CSC/MIS 599, ensuring you meet defense requirements.

Conclusion
By focusing on strategic course selection, academic support, time management, faculty engagement, and personal well-being, you can enhance your academic performance and meet the GPA requirement for your major.
