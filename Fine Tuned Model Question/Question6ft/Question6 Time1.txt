Research or Thesis Requirements:

Final Project Completion: Complete the final project.
Comprehensive Oral Exam: Pass a comprehensive written exam.
Thesis Defense Requirement: Pass a defense of the thesis.
Draft Thesis Submission: Submit a draft thesis electronically to the Graduate School by the deadline.
Format Approval Sheet: Submit this with your draft thesis.
Final Thesis Submission: Submit the final thesis electronically by the deadline.
Signed Thesis Title Page: Include this with your final thesis.
ETD Submission Form: Submit an Electronic Thesis Form with your final thesis.
