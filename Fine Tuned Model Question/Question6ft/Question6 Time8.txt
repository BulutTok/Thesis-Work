Assistant: Here are the additional graduation requirements you need to be aware of:

1. Apply to Graduate: You must apply to graduate in SeaNet by the deadline.

2. Enrollment: Ensure you are enrolled in the semester you plan to graduate.

3. Research Project or Thesis Requirements:
Successfully defend your research project.
Complete the final project.
Pass a comprehensive written exam if applicable.
Pass a defense of the thesis if applicable.
Submit a draft thesis electronically to the Graduate School by the deadline.
Submit a Format Approval Sheet with the draft thesis.
Submit a final thesis electronically by the deadline.
Provide a signed thesis title page with the final thesis.
Submit an Electronic Thesis Form with the final thesis.

Make sure to follow up on these requirements and consult your advisor if you need further clarification or assistance.
