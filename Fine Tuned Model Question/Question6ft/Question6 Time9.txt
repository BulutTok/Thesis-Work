Assistant: To ensure you meet all graduation requirements, please be aware of the following:

Graduation Requirements
Apply to Graduate: You need to apply to graduate in SeaNet by the deadline.
Enroll in Semester of Graduation: Make sure you are enrolled in courses during the semester you plan to graduate.

Research or Thesis Requirements
Defense of Project: Successfully defend your research project.
Final Project Completion: Complete the final project requirements.

Thesis Requirements (if applicable)
Comprehensive Oral Exam: Pass a comprehensive written exam.
Thesis Defense Requirement: Pass a defense of your thesis.
Draft Thesis Submission: Submit a draft thesis electronically to the Graduate School by the deadline.
Format Approval Sheet: Submit a Format Approval Sheet with your draft thesis.
Final Thesis Submission: Submit your final thesis electronically by the deadline.
Signed Thesis Title Page: Include a signed title page with your final thesis submission.
ETD Submission Form: Submit an Electronic Thesis Form with your final thesis.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, it is advisable to consult with your academic advisor or the Registrar's Office.
