Assistant: Core Courses for MScIS Program

Here are the core courses you need to complete for your Master of Science in Computer Science and Information Systems:

1. CSC 532 - Design and Analysis of Algorithms I
Prerequisites: CSC 500

2. MIS 534 - Information Security Management
Prerequisites: BUS 500 and CSC 344 or MIS 323

3. CSC 544 - Network Programming
Prerequisites: CSC 500 and CSC 344 or MIS 323

4. CSC 550 - Software Engineering
Prerequisites: CSC 500 and CSC 450 or MIS 411

5. MIS 555 - Database Management Systems
Prerequisites: BUS 500 and CSC 455 or MIS 315

6. MIS 565 - Analysis, Modeling and Design
Prerequisites: BUS 500 and CSC 450 or MIS 411

Actionable Steps
Ensure you have completed the prerequisite courses before enrolling in each core course.
Plan your schedule to incorporate these prerequisites early in your program if not yet completed.

Summary
These core courses form the foundation of your MScIS degree, with specific prerequisites that must be met prior to enrollment. Prioritize completing any outstanding prerequisites to stay on track with your degree requirements.
