Assistant: Core Courses for MScIS Program

Here are the core courses you need to complete for your Master of Science in Computer Science & Information Systems (MScIS) program:

1. CSC 532 - Design and Analysis of Algorithms I
Prerequisite: None specified.

2. MIS 534 - Information Security Management
Prerequisite: BUS 500 and CSC 344 or MIS 323.

3. CSC 544 - Network Programming
Prerequisite: CSC 500 and CSC 344 or MIS 323.

4. CSC 550 - Software Engineering
Prerequisite: CSC 500 and CSC 450 or MIS 411.

5. MIS 555 - Database Management Systems
Prerequisite: BUS 500 and CSC 455 or MIS 315.

6. MIS 565 - Analysis, Modeling, and Design
Prerequisite: BUS 500 and CSC 450 or MIS 411.
